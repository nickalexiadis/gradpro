About Gradpro

Test, Learn, Improve!

In our rapidly changing world, graduates can expect to change their career than once during their work life. As such a university degree by itself is not adequate for the flexibility that will be needed in the future workforce. Lifelong learning can be made easier through the development of certain "metaskills. And that is exactly what Gradpro is about. An easy-to-use web application that will allow graduates or those already employed to test their metaskills, learn more about them as well as receive guidance as to how to improve them.

Test your skills through a variety of questionnaires

While currently focusing on giving you a general picture of certain metaskills, the addition of new tests in the future will make Gradpro your first destination and hub for continuing education and self- improvement

Learn something new

According to Skills Development Scotland, meta-skills are "higher order skills that create adaptive learners and promote success in whatever context the future brings. These are the skills that enable individuals to perform highly today;". SDC has classified these skills as: Self-Management, Social Intelligence, and Innovation. Each of them can be broken down into individual skills that can be cultivated in various ways.

Improve your meta-skills and ensure a successful career

By testing your skills in the site, you will be able to quickly pinpoint and leverage your strong areas as well as detect skills with room for improvement. Grapdro aims to be your go-to continuing education hub and will be gradually adding more specialized tests, as well as offering more feedback on how to improve in your desired areas.

If you have any questions, need help or would like to see something added to Gradpro, contact us at: gradpro@gmail.com
